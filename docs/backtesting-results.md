## 📌 About Backtesting
Backtesting allows us to evaluate the performance of different **arbitrage betting strategies** on historical data.

Key components analyzed:
- ✅ **Return on Investment (ROI)**
- ✅ **Win/Loss Rate**
- ✅ **Maximum Drawdown**
- ✅ **Risk-Adjusted Performance**
