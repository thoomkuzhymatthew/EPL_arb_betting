## 🎯 **Research Question:**  
**What is the Best Betting Strategy for English Premier League games?**

---

<div class="content-box">
    <h2>📸 Project Overview</h2>
    <img src="harrymaguire.webp" alt="Project Image" width="70%" style="border-radius: 8px;">
    <p><em>Source: OpenAI</em></p>
    
    <h3>What Are We Trying to Do?</h3>
    <p>
        We aim to uncover **the best betting strategy** for **Premier League games**.
        To achieve this, we are building a **backtesting framework** that allows us to simulate
        various betting strategies using **historical EPL data**.
    </p>

    <h3>Key Features of Our Model:</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>🎯 **Strategy Selection:** Choose different betting models.</li>
        <li>💰 **Stake Limits:** Control risk exposure.</li>
        <li>⏳ **Time Period:** Simulate bets over different seasons.</li>
        <li>📊 **Performance Tracking:** Measure profit/loss from a £100 starting fund.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
